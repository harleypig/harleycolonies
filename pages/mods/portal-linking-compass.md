Client/Server: both

**Portal Linking Compass** provides a compass that helps players link
nether portals correctly. The mod displays portal linking information and
helps ensure portals connect to the correct locations in the overworld and
nether.

### Features

- **Portal Linking**: Helps link nether portals correctly.
- **Compass Display**: Shows portal linking information.
- **Coordinate Help**: Assists with portal coordinate calculations.
