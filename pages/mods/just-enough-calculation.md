Client/Server: both

**Just Enough Calculation** adds a calculator interface to JEI (Just
Enough Items) that allows players to calculate how many resources are
needed to craft items in bulk. The mod helps players plan large-scale
crafting projects by calculating ingredient requirements and showing
intermediate steps.

### Features

- **JEI Integration**: Calculator interface integrated into JEI.
- **Bulk Crafting Calculations**: Calculate resources needed for crafting
  multiple items.
- **Recipe Breakdown**: Shows intermediate crafting steps and ingredient
  requirements.
