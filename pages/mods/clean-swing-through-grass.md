**Clean Swing Through Grass** allows players to attack entities through
grass and other foliage blocks without breaking them. The mod improves
combat in grassy areas by allowing attacks to pass through foliage.

### Features

- **Attack Through Foliage**: Attack entities through grass and leaves.
- **Combat Improvement**: Better combat in grassy areas.
- **Non-Destructive**: Foliage blocks are not broken when attacking
  through them.
