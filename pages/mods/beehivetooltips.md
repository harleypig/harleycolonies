**BeehiveTooltips** adds informative tooltips to beehives and bee nests,
displaying honey level, bee count, and other useful information. The mod
makes beekeeping easier by providing clear visual feedback about hive
status.

### Features

- **Hive Information**: Shows honey level and bee count in tooltips.
- **Bee Status**: Displays bee-related information.
- **Beekeeping Aid**: Makes beekeeping more convenient and informative.
