Client/Server: both

**Controlling** enhances the keybind search functionality in Minecraft's
controls menu. The mod provides better search capabilities, shows conflicts
more clearly, and makes it easier to manage keybinds, especially in
modded environments with many keybinds.

### Features

- **Enhanced Search**: Improved keybind search functionality.
- **Conflict Detection**: Better display of keybind conflicts.
- **Keybind Management**: Easier management of keybinds in modded
  environments.
