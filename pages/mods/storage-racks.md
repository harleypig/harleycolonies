**Storage Racks** adds decorative storage racks that can hold items for
display and organization. The mod provides aesthetic storage solutions that
also function as item holders, perfect for organizing and displaying items
in builds.

### Features

- **Decorative Storage**: Storage racks for items.
- **Display Functionality**: Hold and display items.
- **Organization**: Better item organization options.
- **Aesthetic**: Decorative storage solutions.
