Client/Server: both

**Framed Compacting Drawers** adds framed versions of compacting drawers
from Storage Drawers, allowing players to customize the appearance of their
storage drawers. The mod provides aesthetic customization options while
maintaining the functionality of compacting drawers.

### Features

- **Framed Drawers**: Customizable framed compacting drawers.
- **Aesthetic Options**: Various frame materials and styles.
- **Storage Drawers Integration**: Works seamlessly with Storage Drawers
  mod.
