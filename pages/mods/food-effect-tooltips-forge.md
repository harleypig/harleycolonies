Client/Server: both

**Food Effect Tooltips** adds tooltips to food items showing what status
effects they provide when consumed. The mod displays effect duration,
amplifier, and other relevant information, making it easier to choose the
right food for different situations.

### Features

- **Effect Tooltips**: Shows status effects provided by food items.
- **Duration Information**: Displays effect duration and amplifier.
- **Food Planning**: Helps plan meals based on desired effects.
