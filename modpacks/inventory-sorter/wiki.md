---
title: Inventory Sorter
---

**Inventory Sorter** adds automatic inventory sorting functionality to
Minecraft. The mod allows players to quickly organize their inventory and
chests with a single keybind, making inventory management much more
convenient.

### Features

- **One-Key Sorting**: Sort inventory with a single keybind.
- **Chest Sorting**: Sort items in chests and other containers.
- **Customizable**: Configurable sorting behavior and keybinds.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
