---
title: Clumps
---

**Clumps** groups nearby experience orbs together into a single entity,
reducing entity count and improving performance. The mod combines experience
orbs that are close together, making the game run smoother when many
experience orbs are present.

### Features

- **Experience Orb Grouping**: Groups nearby experience orbs together.
- **Performance Improvement**: Reduces entity count for better performance.
- **Seamless Integration**: Works transparently without affecting gameplay.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
