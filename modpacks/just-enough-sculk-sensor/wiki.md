---
title: Just Enough Sculk Sensor
---

**Just Enough Sculk Sensor** adds sculk sensor information to JEI (Just
Enough Items), displaying what vibrations trigger sculk sensors. The mod
helps players understand sculk sensor mechanics and plan redstone
contraptions using sculk sensors.

### Features

- **Sculk Sensor Info**: Information about sculk sensor triggers in JEI.
- **Vibration Details**: Shows what vibrations activate sculk sensors.
- **Redstone Planning**: Helps plan sculk sensor-based contraptions.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
- harleycolonies-1.20.1-0.1.2
