---
title: Actually Harvest (right click and XP)
---

**Actually Harvest** allows players to right-click crops to harvest them
without breaking the block, and automatically replants seeds. The mod also
provides XP for harvesting crops, making farming more convenient and
rewarding.

### Features

- **Right-Click Harvesting**: Harvest crops by right-clicking.
- **Auto-Replant**: Automatically replants seeds after harvesting.
- **XP Rewards**: Provides experience points for harvesting crops.
- **Convenient Farming**: Makes farming faster and more efficient.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
