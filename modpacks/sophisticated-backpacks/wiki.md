---
title: Sophisticated Backpacks
---

**Sophisticated Backpacks** adds a variety of backpacks with different
sizes and upgrade options. The mod provides portable storage solutions
with features like auto-pickup, filtering, and various upgrades to enhance
backpack functionality.

### Features

- **Multiple Backpack Tiers**: Different backpack sizes and materials.
- **Upgrades**: Various upgrade options to enhance backpack functionality.
- **Auto-Pickup**: Automatic item collection features.
- **Filtering**: Item filtering and organization options.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
