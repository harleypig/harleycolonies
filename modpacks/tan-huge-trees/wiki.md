---
title: Tan's Huge Trees
---

**Tan's Huge Trees** adds massive tree structures to Minecraft worlds,
creating impressive natural landmarks. The mod generates huge trees of
various types, adding visual variety and interesting structures to explore.

### Features

- **Huge Trees**: Massive tree structures generated in the world.
- **Variety**: Different types of huge trees.
- **World Generation**: Natural integration into world generation.

### Modpacks

This mod is installed in the following modpacks:

- harleycolonies-1.21.1
